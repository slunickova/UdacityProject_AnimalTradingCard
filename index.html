Earthworm

Earthworms have enormous impact on soil fertility and hereby to human's wellbeing.

  • Scientific Name: Lumbricus terrestris
  • Avarage Length: 20 - 25 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 6 years
  • Habitat: Eurasia

This worm species is quite common around the globe. It is discreet, everyone knows it, it doesn't seem to be anything special. However its populations have great ipact on our lives. In soil it turns organic matter into hummus, thus improving soil fertility. Protect your common earthworm to enjoy healthy veggies from your backyard!
